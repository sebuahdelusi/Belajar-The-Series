## 1. Pengenalan Day Trading

### 1.1 Apa Itu Day Trading?
Day trading adalah aktivitas membeli dan menjual instrumen keuangan (saham) dalam satu hari perdagangan yang sama. Posisi tidak dibawa overnight (menginap). Tujuannya adalah mengambil keuntungan dari pergerakan harga jangka pendek.

**Karakteristik Day Trader:**
- Membuka dan menutup posisi dalam hari yang sama
- Fokus pada volatilitas harga jangka pendek
- Menggunakan leverage (margin) untuk memperbesar potensi keuntungan
- Membutuhkan waktu penuh untuk memantau pasar
- Melakukan banyak transaksi dalam sehari

### 1.2 Perbedaan dengan Investasi Jangka Panjang

| Aspek | Day Trading | Investasi Jangka Panjang |
|-------|-------------|--------------------------|
| **Durasi Holding** | Intraday (< 1 hari) | Bulanan hingga tahunan |
| **Analisis Utama** | Teknikal | Fundamental |
| **Frekuensi Trading** | Sangat tinggi | Rendah |
| **Risiko** | Tinggi | Sedang-Rendah |
| **Waktu yang Dibutuhkan** | Full-time | Part-time |
| **Biaya Transaksi** | Tinggi | Rendah |

### 5.2 Support & Resistance

**Support:** Level harga di mana tekanan beli cukup kuat untuk mencegah harga turun lebih jauh.

**Resistance:** Level harga di mana tekanan jual cukup kuat untuk mencegah harga naik lebih jauh.

**Cara Identifikasi:**
1. Cari level di mana harga berulang kali berbalik arah
2. Previous high/low
3. Round numbers (psikologis)
4. Fibonacci levels
5. Moving averages

**Trading pada S/R:**
- **Bouncing:** Buy di support, sell di resistance
- **Breakout:** Buy saat break resistance, sell saat break support
- **Fakeout:** Waspadai false breakout

### 5.3 Trend

**Jenis Trend:**
1. **Uptrend:** Higher highs + higher lows
2. **Downtrend:** Lower highs + lower lows
3. **Sideways:** Range-bound

**Prinsip:**
- "Trend is your friend"
- Trading searah trend lebih aman
- Identifikasi trend di multiple timeframes

### 5.4 Indikator Teknikal

**1. Moving Average (MA)**
```
SMA = (P1 + P2 + ... + Pn) / n
EMA = (Price Ã— k) + (EMA_yesterday Ã— (1-k))
     where k = 2/(n+1)
```

**Penggunaan:**
- Identifikasi trend
- Support/Resistance dinamis
- Golden cross (MA50 cross above MA200) = bullish
- Death cross (MA50 cross below MA200) = bearish

**Popular MA:**
- MA5, MA10, MA20 (short-term)
- MA50, MA100, MA200 (long-term)

**2. Relative Strength Index (RSI)**
```
RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
RS = Average Gain / Average Loss
```

**Interpretasi:**
- RSI > 70: Overbought (potensi koreksi)
- RSI < 30: Oversold (potensi rebound)
- RSI 50: Neutral
- Divergence: Sinyal reversal

**3. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)**
```
MACD Line = EMA12 - EMA26
Signal Line = EMA9 of MACD
Histogram = MACD - Signal
```

**Sinyal:**
- MACD cross above signal = bullish
- MACD cross below signal = bearish
- Histogram mengembang = trend menguat
- Divergence = potensi reversal

**4. Bollinger Bands**
```
Middle Band = SMA20
Upper Band = SMA20 + (2 Ã— STD)
Lower Band = SMA20 - (2 Ã— STD)
```

**Trading Strategy:**
- Price touch lower band = oversold (buy signal)
- Price touch upper band = overbought (sell signal)
- Squeeze = volatilitas rendah, bersiap breakout
- Expansion = volatilitas tinggi

**5. Volume**
- Konfirmasi trend dan breakout
- Volume meningkat saat breakout = valid
- Volume rendah saat breakout = false breakout
- Volume spike = perhatian khusus

**6. Stochastic Oscillator**

## 6. Strategi Day Trading

### 6.1 Scalping

**Karakteristik:**
- Hold time: Detik hingga menit
- Target profit: 0.5% - 1%
- Frekuensi: Sangat tinggi (10-100+ trades/day)
- Risk: Rendah per trade, tinggi akumulatif

**Setup:**
- Timeframe: 1min, 5min
- Indikator: MA5, MA10, VWAP
- Focus: Spread, liquidity, execution speed

**Strategi:**
1. **MA Crossover Scalping**
   - Entry: MA5 cross MA10
   - Exit: Opposite cross atau target 0.5%
   - Stop: 0.3%

2. **VWAP Scalping**
   - Buy: Price dip below VWAP, bounce back
   - Sell: Price spike above VWAP, reject
   - Stop: 0.5% dari VWAP

**Keuntungan:**
- Profit konsisten kecil-kecil
- Exposure minimal

**Kekurangan:**
- Stress tinggi
- Biaya transaksi besar
- Butuh fokus penuh

### 6.2 Momentum Trading

**Karakteristik:**
- Hold time: Menit hingga jam
- Target profit: 2% - 5%
- Frekuensi: 3-10 trades/day
- Focus: Saham dengan momentum kuat

**Cara Identifikasi Momentum:**
- Volume spike (>2x average)
- Price breakout dengan volume
- News catalyst
- Gap up/down di open

**Strategi:**
1. **Breakout Momentum**
   - Entry: Breakout resistance dengan volume tinggi
   - Confirmation: Retest breakout level hold
   - Exit: Profit target 3-5% atau trailing stop
   - Stop: Below breakout level

2. **Gap & Go**
   - Entry: Gap up >3% dengan volume
   - Confirmation: First 5min candle bullish
   - Exit: Saat momentum melemah (volume turun)
   - Stop: Below opening range low

**Screening Criteria:**
```
- Volume > 2x average
- Price change > 3%
- Price > Rp 500 (spread rendah)
- Market cap > Rp 1T (liquidity)
- Float < 50% (easier to move)
```

### 6.3 Reversal Trading

**Karakteristik:**
- Hold time: Jam
- Target profit: 3% - 7%
- Frekuensi: 2-5 trades/day
- Risk: Sedang-tinggi (counter-trend)

**Setup:**
1. **Oversold Bounce**
   - Saham turun tajam (>5%)
   - RSI < 30
   - Volume climax (selling exhaustion)
   - Entry: Saat first bullish candle
   - Stop: Below recent low

2. **Overbought Fade**
   - Saham naik tajam (>5%)
   - RSI > 70
   - Rejection di resistance
   - Entry: Saat first bearish candle
   - Stop: Above recent high

**Konfirmasi Reversal:**
- Divergence (price vs RSI/MACD)
- Candlestick reversal pattern
- Support/resistance level
- Volume confirmation

### 6.4 Range Trading

**Karakteristik:**
- Market: Sideways/ranging
- Target: Range width
- Entry: Support/resistance
- Exit: Opposite side

**Strategi:**
1. Identifikasi support & resistance jelas
2. Buy di support dengan konfirmasi
3. Sell di resistance dengan konfirmasi
4. Stop loss di luar range
5. Exit jika breakout (range berakhir)

**Indikator Pendukung:**
- Bollinger Bands
- Stochastic
- RSI

### 6.5 News Trading

**Karakteristik:**
- Trigger: Corporate action, earning, news
- Volatilitas: Sangat tinggi
- Risk: Tinggi
- Reward: Tinggi

**Jenis News:**
1. **Pre-market News**
   - Earning report
   - Corporate action
   - Economic data
   - Trade sebelum market open

2. **Intraday News**
   - Breaking news
   - Rumor
   - Analyst upgrade/downgrade

**Strategi:**
- **Play the News:** Trade searah sentiment
- **Fade the News:** Counter-trade jika overreaction
- **Wait & See:** Tunggu initial volatility mereda

### 6.6 Opening Range Breakout (ORB)

**Setup:**
1. Catat high & low di 15-30 menit pertama
2. Entry saat breakout range tersebut
3. Target: 1-2x range
4. Stop: Opposite side of range

**Best Condition:**
- Gap opening
- High premarket volume
- Strong catalyst

## 7. Manajemen Risiko

### 7.1 Position Sizing

**Formula:**
```
Position Size = (Account Ã— Risk%) / (Entry - Stop Loss)

Contoh:
Account: Rp 50.000.000
Risk per trade: 1% = Rp 500.000
Entry: Rp 1.000
Stop loss: Rp 970 (3% dari entry)
Risk per share: Rp 30

Position Size = Rp 500.000 / Rp 30 = 16.666 shares â‰ˆ 166 lot
```

**Rules:**
- Risk per trade: 0.5% - 2% dari modal
- Max position: 10-20% dari modal per saham
- Max daily loss: 3-5% dari modal
- Diversifikasi: Max 3-5 posisi concurrent

### 7.2 Risk-Reward Ratio

**Minimum R:R = 1:2**
```
Risk: Rp 100.000 (1%)
Reward: Rp 200.000 (2%)

Break-even dengan win rate 35%:
Win: 35% Ã— Rp 200.000 = Rp 70.000
Loss: 65% Ã— Rp 100.000 = Rp 65.000
Net: +Rp 5.000
```

**Perhitungan:**
- R:R 1:1 â†’ Need 50% win rate
- R:R 1:2 â†’ Need 35% win rate
- R:R 1:3 â†’ Need 26% win rate

### 7.3 Stop Loss

**Tipe Stop Loss:**

1. **Percentage-based**
   - Fix percentage (2-3%)
   - Simple & consistent
   - Tidak memperhitungkan technical

2. **Technical-based**
   - Below support/above resistance
   - Below/above swing low/high
   - Below/above MA
   - Lebih logical

3. **Time-based**
   - Exit jika tidak bergerak dalam X menit
   - Cut loss di end of day

4. **Trailing Stop**
   - Stop loss bergerak mengikuti profit
   - Protect profit sambil biarkan winner run
   - Contoh: Trail 50% dari peak profit

**Rules:**
- SELALU pasang stop loss sebelum entry
- Jangan pindah stop loss jika sudah set
- Exit immediately jika stop hit
- No mercy, no hope, just execute

### 7.4 Take Profit Strategy

**Methods:**

1. **Fixed Target**
   - Target fix (misal 3%)
   - Exit semua posisi

2. **Scaling Out**
   - Exit sebagian di target 1
   - Move stop to breakeven
   - Exit sisanya di target 2
   - Contoh: 50% @ 2%, 50% @ 4%

3. **Trailing Stop**
   - Biarkan winner run
   - Stop bergerak mengikuti harga
   - Exit jika reverse X%

4. **Technical-based**
   - Exit di resistance
   - Exit saat indikator reversal
   - Exit saat pattern complete

### 7.5 Rules Emas Risk Management

1. **Never risk more than 1-2% per trade**
2. **Always use stop loss**
3. **Risk-reward minimum 1:2**
4. **Cut losers quick, let winners run**
5. **Max daily loss 3-5%** â†’ Stop trading hari itu
6. **Avoid revenge trading**
7. **Position sizing discipline**
8. **Diversifikasi** (jangan all-in satu saham)
9. **No trading saat emotional**
10. **Journal every trade**

## 8. Psikologi Trading

### 8.1 Emosi dalam Trading

**Fear (Ketakutan):**
- Takut loss â†’ Tidak execute signal
- Takut miss out â†’ FOMO entry
- Takut profit hilang â†’ Close terlalu cepat

**Greed (Keserakahan):**
- Tidak take profit â†’ Balik loss
- Overtrading â†’ Burn account
- Overleveraging â†’ Blown account

**Hope (Harapan):**
- Tidak cut loss â†’ Loss membesar
- Averaging down tanpa plan â†’ Deeper loss
- Gambling mentality

**Revenge (Balas Dendam):**
- Trading untuk balik loss
- Tidak ikuti plan
- Emotional decision
- Biasanya result: Loss lebih besar

### 8.2 Cognitive Bias

**1. Confirmation Bias**
- Cari info yang support posisi kita
- Ignore info yang berlawanan
- Solution: Objective analysis, both sides

**2. Recency Bias**
- Terlalu fokus pada recent events
- Asumsi trend akan continue
- Solution: Long-term perspective

**3. Loss Aversion**
- Takut loss > cinta profit
- Hold loser, cut winner
- Solution: Objective exit rules

**4. Overconfidence**
- Setelah winning streak
- Anggap remeh risk
- Solution: Stay humble, follow plan

**5. Gambler's Fallacy**
- "Sudah 5x loss, pasti next win"
- Setiap trade independent
- Solution: Probabilistic thinking

### 8.3 Disiplin Trading

**Trading Plan:**
```
1. Pre-Market:
   - Watchlist saham
   - Setup yang dicari
   - Entry/exit criteria
   - Position size
   - Risk limit

2. During Market:
   - Follow plan strictly
   - No impulsive trade
   - Take break jika loss streak

3. Post-Market:
   - Journal all trades
   - Analyze mistakes
   - Prepare next day
```

**Checklist Sebelum Entry:**
- [ ] Setup sesuai strategy?
- [ ] Position size sudah dihitung?
- [ ] Stop loss sudah ditentukan?
- [ ] Risk-reward acceptable?
- [ ] Tidak sedang emotional?
- [ ] Belum exceed daily loss limit?

### 8.4 Dealing dengan Loss

**Acceptance:**
- Loss adalah bagian dari trading
- Tidak ada trader 100% win rate
- Yang penting: Total profit > total loss

**Evaluasi:**
- Was it a mistake atau probability?
- Follow plan atau emotional?
- What can be learned?

**Recovery:**
- Jangan revenge trade
- Take break if needed
- Back to small size
- Rebuild confidence gradually

### 8.5 Winning Mindset

**Growth Mindset:**
- Continuous learning
- Embrace mistakes as lessons
- Adapt and improve

**Process over Outcome:**
- Focus pada execute plan dengan baik
- Bukan focus pada profit/loss
- Good process â†’ good outcome long-term

**Patience:**
- Wait for right setup
- Don't force trade
- Quality > quantity

**Detachment:**
- Jangan attach emotionally ke posisi
- Just business decision
- Cut loss tanpa ego

### 9.5 Backtesting

**Purpose:**
- Test strategy effectiveness
- Understand win rate & expectancy
- Refine parameters
- Build confidence

**How to Backtest:**

2. Test on historical data (min 100 trades)
3. Record all trades
4. Calculate metrics:
   - Win rate
   - Average R:R
   - Expectancy
   - Max drawdown
   - Profit factor

**Metrics:**
